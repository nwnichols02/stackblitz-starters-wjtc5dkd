Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our memberships and policies.

How do I sign up for a membership?

You can sign up online by adding your desired plan to your cart and completing the checkout process. Alternatively, visit us in-studio during business hours and our staff will help you get started. All new members receive a complimentary orientation session.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! All our monthly memberships are commitment-free. You can cancel anytime by notifying us at least 7 days before your next billing date. There are no cancellation fees or penalties. We want you to stay because you love it, not because you're locked in.

Can I freeze my membership?

Absolutely! Life happens, and we understand. You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for travel, injury, or other reasons. Just let us know at least 5 days before your billing date. There's no fee to freeze your account.

What's the difference between memberships and class passes?

Memberships offer unlimited access and renew monthly, making them ideal for regular attendees. Class passes provide a set number of classes with an expiration date, perfect for those who prefer flexibility. Members also get additional perks like priority booking and equipment discounts.

Do class passes expire?

Yes, the 10-Class Pass is valid for 3 months from the date of purchase. We recommend this option if you plan to attend at least 3-4 classes per month. If you need more time, contact us—we're happy to work with you on extensions for valid reasons.

Can I share my membership with family members?

Monthly memberships are individual and non-transferable. However, the 10-Class Pass can be shared among immediate family members living in the same household. Each person using the pass must be registered in our system for safety reasons.

What should I bring to my first class?

Bring comfortable workout clothing, a water bottle, and a positive attitude! We provide all necessary equipment including yoga mats, weights, and props. For Pilates reformer classes, grip socks are required (available for purchase at the studio). Arrive 10-15 minutes early for new member orientation.

Is there a trial period or free class?

New to ABC Fitness? Your first class is on us! Simply contact us or stop by the studio to claim your free trial class. This allows you to experience our community and instruction style before committing to a membership or pass.
